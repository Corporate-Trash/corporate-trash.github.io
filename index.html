CORPORATE TRASH

For decades now, corporations have been carelessly dumping their waste and contaminants in New Zealand's waters. This breaches the conditions of their trade waste consents - If they had any to begin with.

In all this time, regional councils have done next to nothing to stop them. In fact, some don't even bother monitoring what corporations dump in the sewers, instead letting the corporations handle that themselves. And thanks to a legal loophole, none of these corporations have been fined for their atrocious behaviour.

Simply put, this is ridiculous. Our environment is already fragile, and we can't keep letting the corporations get away with this.

Want to help? Sign this petition demanding our councils take action and start monitoring more closely! Our goal is to reach 5,000 signatures before the 26th of June, when the petition will be submitted to the New Zealand Parliament.
